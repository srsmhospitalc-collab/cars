2026 Me India Ki Top 5 Budget Cars - Puri Jankari Mileage Aur Features Ke Sath

Note: Ye article samanya jankari ke liye hai. Car ki keemat aur features shehar ke hisab se alag ho sakte hain. Kharidne se pehle dealership par confirm kar lein.

Dosto, agar aap 2026 me 10 lakh ke budget me nayi car lene ka soch rahe ho to ye article aapke liye hai. Humne market ki 5 sabse value-for-money cars chuni hain jo mileage, safety aur features me best hain. Pura article padhiye aur apne liye best car chune.

Car Kharidne Se Pehle Ye 3 Baat Dhyan Rakhein

Nayi car lena ek bada faisla hai. Sirf keemat dekhkar car mat kharidein. In 3 cheezon par zarur dhyan dein:

1. Mileage Kitna Hai?

Petrol ki keemat roz badh rahi hai. Isliye aisi car chune jo 20 kmpl se zyada mileage de. CNG option wali car bhi best hai agar aapka roz ka chalna 50km se zyada hai.

2. Safety Rating Kya Hai?

Global NCAP me 4 ya 5 star rating wali car hi lein. Aapki aur family ki jaan sabse keemti hai. Airbags, ABS jaise features basic hone chahiye.

3. Service Cost Kam Ho

Maruti, Hyundai jaisi company ki service sasti padti hai aur parts har jagah mil jate hain. Koi nayi company ki car loge to service me dikkat aa sakti hai.

1. Maruti Suzuki Swift 2026

₹6.49 - 9.64 Lakh

Swift India ki sabse pasandida hatchback hai. Naya 2026 model aur bhi zyada stylish aur feature-loaded hai. Shahar me chalane ke liye ye best hai.

Key Features:

Engine: 1.2L Petrol + CNG Option
Mileage: 24.8 kmpl Petrol, 32.8 km/kg CNG
Safety: 6 Airbags, ESP, Hill Hold
Special: 9-inch Touchscreen, Wireless Charger, 360 Camera

Kisko Leni Chahiye: Agar aap pehli baar car kharid rahe ho ya aapko roz office jana hai traffic me, to Swift se behtar kuch nahi. Chhoti hai to park karne me aasan, aur Maruti ki service to har gali me hai.

Kami: Peeche ki seat par 3 mote log baithne me dikkat hogi. Boot space bhi 265L hi hai.

2. Tata Punch EV

₹10.99 - 14.44 Lakh

Petrol mehenga lagta hai? To ye Electric car dekho. Punch EV budget me best electric SUV hai. Ek baar charge karo aur 300km tak tension free chalo.

Key Features:

Battery: 25 kWh ya 35 kWh Option
Range: 315km se 421km tak
Safety: 5 Star Bharat NCAP Rating
Special: Ventilated Seats, Sunroof, 10.25-inch Display

Kisko Leni Chahiye: Jinka roz ka chalna 40-50 km hai aur ghar me charging point lagwa sakte hain. 8 saal ki battery warranty milti hai. Petrol ka kharcha 80% tak kam ho jayega.

Kami: Highway pe charging station dhundne padenge. Long trip me rukna pad sakta hai.

3. Hyundai Exter

₹6.21 - 10.42 Lakh

Exter ek "Micro SUV" hai. Matlab hatchback ke price me SUV wali feeling deti hai. Ground clearance zyada hai to kharab rasta me bhi chinta nahi.

Key Features:

Engine: 1.2L Petrol + CNG
Mileage: 19.4 kmpl, 27.1 km/kg CNG
Safety: 6 Airbags Standard, Dashcam
Special: Sunroof, Voice Commands, 8-inch Screen

Kisko Leni Chahiye: Family ke liye car chahiye aur kabhi-kabhi gaon jana padta hai to Exter lo. Unchi hai to road dikhta hai acche se.
